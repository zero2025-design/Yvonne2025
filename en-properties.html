My health is my decision

Shanghai Liangfeng Oil has been deeply engaged in the oil industry for 27 years, advancing steadily with a long-term vision. From successfully becoming an authorized distributor of Sinograin Oils to joining hands with Polyoil, a Canadian oil giant, to co-found the "Yirongfang" brand, its original aspiration of "making high-quality oil with dedication" has never wavered and has become a core belief engraved in the company's development.

 

With a global perspective, we integrate resources from the global supply chain to incorporate world-class quality into our product system. Leveraging the core "nutrient and freshness locking" technology as a solid guarantee, we conduct comprehensive and meticulous quality control over every drop of oil. More importantly, the concept of "healthy family life" has been deeply integrated into our brand DNA, running through every key stage of our development — from the in-depth market cultivation and reputation building of Haishi packaged edible oil to the precise introduction and promotion of high-quality oils such as linseed oil. Every decision and practice embodies our pursuit of ultimate quality and rigorous attitude.

Bioriginal Food & Science Corp., Canada


Accreditations & Quality Assurance

Bioriginal Food & Science Corp., Canada is a global leader in providing comprehensive Omega solutions for the food and nutraceutical industries. With over two decades of specialized expertise in extracting Omega ingredients, the company pioneered its first Omega product—borage oil—in 1993 by integrating the entire value chain. In 1998, it launched its second Omega ingredient—flaxseed oil. At the time, Bioriginal was among the earliest companies in North America to implement blending technology within its production facilities. In fact, Bioriginal also became the first company in North America to develop a health supplement blending Omega 3-6-9 from borage oil, flaxseed oil, and fish oil. Its product range and production capacity rank among the largest in Canada.Within the North American health food sector, Bioriginal Food & Science Corp. plays a pivotal role in Omega research, authoring publications and establishing industry standards for Omega products. The company holds international certifications including HACCP, GMP, Halal, Kosher, and Canadian Organic.
